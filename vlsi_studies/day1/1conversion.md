Chapter-1 Digital system and Binary Numbers

Conversions:
            Decimal ---> Binary(/2)
            Decimal ---> Octal(/8)
            Decimal ---> Hexadecimal(/16)
            Binary ---> Decimal(*2^n)
            Binary ---> Octal(Group 3 bits)
            Binary ---> Hexadecimal(Group 4 bits)
            Octal ---> Binary(Replace octal bit by 3 bit binary)
            Octal ---> Decimal(*8^n)
            Octal ---> Hexadecimal
               |---> Binary--->|
            Hexadecimal ---> Decimal(*16^n)
            Hexadecimal ---> Binary(Replace each bit by 4 bit binary)
            Hexadecimal ---> Octal
                 |--->Binary--->|
